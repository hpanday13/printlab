# Combining Technology and History

We combine computational technology with historical research to create unique insights into the historical and cultural layers present in Japanese landscape prints.

## Platform Features

### Interactive Mapping
Our custom-built interface allows you to align historical prints with modern terrain models.

### AI-Assisted Analysis
Advanced image recognition helps identify landscape features and visual patterns.

### Collaborative Tools
Work together with other participants to verify locations and share discoveries.
